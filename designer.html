For interior designers

Why is it beneficial for interior designers to cooperate with us?

Challenges Faced by Interior Designers

Interior designers often encounter various obstacles during their work. Here, we will outline the main challenges and demonstrate how collaboration with Fillico can help minimize these issues.

1 Finding Clients

Problem: Where to find clients?

Solution: In addition to traditional methods like advertising and marketing, Fillico suggests connecting with furniture manufacturers. Occasionally, we receive inquiries to bring their concepts to life even when a design project isn't already in place. We would be delighted to include you in our directory of designers that we can recommend to our clients.

2 Communication with Clients

Problem: Communication with clients.

Solution: Designers and clients often have vastly different tastes. Striking a balance between your artistic vision and the client's desires can be a difficult task. While seasoned designers may find it simpler to express their views, others might need to develop this ability. Fillico organizes lectures and workshops for emerging designers and architects, where speakers reveal tips on how to engage with clients successfully. We also explore methods for assessing clients and comprehending their requirements.

3 Problems and fears

Problem: How to work for yourself?

Solution: In a studio, designers often focus on a specific set of tasks. Managing the client is not always necessary due to the effective division of labor among colleagues. However, when ambitions become overwhelming, those working independently must either master the entire process themselves or seek a reliable team of specialists. Regardless of the path you choose, know that at Fillico, there are experienced technical designers and engineers who will always be your safety net in case of mistakes. In this section, you will also find answers to additional questions related to marketing, sales, documentation, and handling crisis situations with clients.

4 Personal brand

Problem: How to get a reputation

Solution: The stronger the brand, the louder your voice resonates. However, building a name for yourself is not a quick endeavor. Relying solely on intuition without a clear plan and the necessary knowledge can lead to prolonged pursuit of goals. For instance, a reputation as a reliable professional must be earned by adhering to deadlines and budgets, as well as demonstrating flexibility in unforeseen circumstances. You can gain knowledge through our seminars. We invite renowned experts and hold free meetings for aspiring designers.

5 Examples of success

Problem: The career path is full of mistakes.

Solution: We will help you avoid mistakes, because we have extensive experience. You will get this experience by collaborating with us.
